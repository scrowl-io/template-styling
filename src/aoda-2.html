Module Objectives

Explain AODA Compliance Requirements

General Requirements

While each of the five IASR standards has its own specific requirements, there are also general requirements that apply to all of the standards.

These include:

  • Training
  • Accessibility policy
  • Accessibility plan

Accessibility Training

AODA training gives employees basic knowledge about accessibility and how it impacts people with disabilities.

tips_and_updates

Who needs AODA Training?

AODA training is required if you are a paid worker, a volunteer, or a new worker.

Record Keeping

All organizations have to keep track of their workers' training records, which must include the name of the worker and date of completion.

Accessibility Policy

  • Providing goods
  • Services
  • Facilities

All large private and non-profit organizations and all public-sector organizations must have an accessibility policy in writing. These sectors must also make their policies available to the public.

Accessibility Plan

The plan outlines:

  1. What steps an organization will take to prevent and remove barriers to employment
  2. Organizations must also publish annual status reports
  3. Review and update their plans at least every five years
